Influence - 2017

Our first EP "Influence" was the perfect way for us to start building our identity as a band. It allowed us to develop our mixing skills in the studio, while allowing us to play concerts, reinforcing our stage presence and group cohesion. The EP is made up of five songs, inspired by the bands that brpught us together during their writing. The songs are available on all the usual platforms, and you can buy the EP via Paypal.
