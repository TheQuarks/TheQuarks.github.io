Formed in 2016, The Quarks are a group of rock lovers based around Grenoble, France. We have a vast array of musical preferences, from classical music to metal, which ensures a rich base upon which we compose. Our debut EP "Influence" can be seen as an attempt to condense this musical heritage into a single project. The fact that we do as much as possible ourselves, from writing to recording and mixing, allows us to push ourselves to our limits and get a result that we can truly be proud of.

On the fourth of May 2017, we won the Trema Battle of the bands in Voiron, and were awarded a day of professional coaching and a concert headlining at the Grand Angle. These have allowed us build a powerful live sound and strong stage presence.
